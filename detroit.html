Burger Battle 2018

Detroit

What is a burger battle?

The Burger Battle is a celebration of one of the most exciting and growing restaurant and bar cultures in the country. Join over 1000 burger lovers for the opportunity to sample and taste burgers from over 25 of the best restaurants in Detroit. Each ticket entitles you to All You Can Sample Burgers from your choice of restaurants. Each restaurant/chef will also be selling additional burgers (price to be determined by the vendor) and offering all of their creations for your enjoyment. There will also be a full bar, beer, and non-alcoholic sodas and bottled water available for purchase. Entertainment will be provided by one of Detroit’s Top DJs, and the event will also feature a corn hole tournament and a full kids playground and interactive experience. Don’t forget to also take a look at the 10+ merchants offerings while enjoying your favorite burger along with some great interactive games to enjoy! Finalists will prepare their best burgers for our 5 member celebrity judges panel to be declared Detroit’s Burger Battle Champion 2018.
